Hello, I’m Huyen.

Portland, Oregon.

Summary

I’m an aspiring digital nomad and suitcase entrepreneur who loves everything about the web, digital marketing and the startup culture. I've lived in the pristine Pacific Northwest region of the United States for 20 years and have worked in lots of different jobs in different sectors such as mental health, academic research, retail, and education before discovering technology and entrepreneurship. I’m excited to bring my life experience to the process of building awesome web apps.

I’ve been a jack-of-all-trades at various startups in cross-functional roles such as marketing, researcher, project manager, operations, and community manager and am a life-long learner who's always interested in expanding my skills and transitioning into a front-end engineer.

Goals

I want to master the process of building modern web apps and increase my knowledge, skills and abilities in:

  • HTML5
  • CSS3
  • JavaScript
  • jQuery
  • React.js

I’d like to work for a tech company that endorses the distributive team model of Buffer and Automattic and grow into a customer experience engineer while pursuiting my interests in travel, personal growth and internet entreprenuership through blogging.
