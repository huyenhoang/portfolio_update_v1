My 2 Favorite Treehouse Teachers

Joy Kesten

Joy makes you feel like a kid in an elementary classroom again. The way she communicate concepts makes you feel reassured that the desert of despair is a myth.

Dave McFarland

I first met Dave a year ago at a Treehouse Meetup. I needed help installing a forum called Discourse onto a website for the startup I was with at the time. None of us knew a lick of code and it was expensive to find a developer. Dave helped me install the forum. I didn't know how to bring up the terminal on my Mac until Dave showed me. Shortly later, I decided to learn how to code on Treehouse and Dave has been one of my favorite JavaScript teachers. He sounds like he's on helium when I set the videos at 2x speed.
